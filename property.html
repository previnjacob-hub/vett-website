Residential & Apartments

WHAT WE CHECK —
INSIDE AND OUT.

Apartments, independent houses, and villas. Structural inspection + independent legal review in one report.

🏗️ Structural Condition

  • Walls — cracks, damp, paint masking issues
  • Ceiling — sag, water stains, seepage
  • Floors — sinking, water damage, hollow tiles
  • Overall build quality assessment

🔧 Plumbing & Electrical

  • All taps — water pressure and clarity
  • Drain speed test — all outlets
  • Outlet testing — live power confirmed
  • Electrical panel and wiring check
  • Water meter check — hidden leak detection

🚪 Fittings & Fixtures

  • All doors — open, close, lock tested
  • All windows — seal, lock, glass condition
  • Kitchen cabinets and wardrobes
  • Balcony railing stability

🏢 Building & Environment

  • Lobby, elevator, stairwell condition
  • Parking and security infrastructure
  • Natural light and ventilation
  • Odour, mould, and pest check
  • Neighbourhood and access assessment

📄 Legal Documents

  • Title deed and ownership chain
  • Encumbrance certificate
  • Building approvals and NOCs
  • Possession certificate
  • Occupancy certificate where applicable

📸 Evidence Collected

  • 40–50+ timestamped photos
  • Video walkthrough of entire unit
  • GPS location stamp
  • Document photos for advocate review
  • Signed declaration from inspector
Land & Plot

BEFORE YOU BUY
LAND — VERIFY IT.

Boundary walk, access road, legal title. Everything on the ground and in the documents — independently checked.

📍 Boundary Verification

  • GPS-verified boundary walk — all 4 sides
  • Neighbour confirmation on each boundary
  • Boundary stones and markers checked
  • Encroachment identified and documented
  • Mandatory boundary video — full perimeter

🛣️ Access & Road

  • Road type — public, panchayat, private
  • Vehicle accessibility confirmed
  • Access dispute check
  • Video from public road to entrance

🌿 Physical Condition

  • Terrain type and slope
  • Waterlogging and drainage assessment
  • Water body proximity
  • Natural vs filled land verification
  • Existing structures documented

📄 Legal Documents

  • Title deed and ownership chain
  • Encumbrance certificate
  • Possession certificate
  • Land tax receipt and records
  • Location map and survey number
  • Court and litigation search (Premium)
